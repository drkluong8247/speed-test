Race to the Hospital

  • High Concept: Rushing along a road to beat the timer.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player can enjoy a simple side-scrolling fast-paced drive.
  • Genre: Side-Scrolling Racing game
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys to move. Beat the timer and reach the end of the game.
  • Prototype goal: To get the mechanic of changing speeds to work.
  • Features: ....you get to drive a flying ambulance? not much else unique yet.
  • Setting/Premise: In a world sometime in the future, flying vehichles have been invented. Of course, there are traffic laws to go along with these, and roads still exist, because demolishing them takes a lot of effort. In any case, vehicles can fly now. Of course, the first flying vehicles to be created were emergency vehicles, like ambulances and fire trucks. Instead of having cars pull over when they pass, better to just skip over them entirely.
  • Story: You've just picked up a patient who was in a terrible car accident. If you thought car accidents were bad before, imagine flying car accidents. I mean, you crash, and then you tumble out of the sky and hit the ground. ouch. The patient is in serious need of recieving a replacement organ, but if the patient doesn't recieve it soon, things will not end well. Race through the skies to reach the hospital and save your patient!
  • Target Audience: For people that enjoy racing a vehicle.
  • Play time: Timer is set for 2 minutes. Any longer, and the player loses instantly.
  • Strategies: Don't hit other cars.
  • Prototype Assets: MS Paint was used to make the sprites and background from scratch. used youtube to find the sound, uploaded by yovilleactressselena.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Borrowed from the Phaser Example - tanks and Phaser Example - Invaders to create the enemies and player controls.
